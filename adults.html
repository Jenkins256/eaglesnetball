Join the Eagles

Whether you're a seasoned played or completely new to the game, we welcome all women!

The Eagles Netball Club is more than just a sports team - it's a community of enthusiastic, supportive individuals who share a common passion for netball. We’re not just about the game; we’re about the friendships made, the shared triumphs, the learnings from defeats, and the journey we all embark upon together. Join us and experience the rush of competition, the joy of personal development, and the sense of belonging that comes with being part of the Eagles family. The court is calling. Are you ready to answer?
