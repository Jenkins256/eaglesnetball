Eagles Juniors

Catering for ages 12 - 15

Bishop Ramsey CoE School, Hume Way, Ruislip, HA4 8EE

Not only do we aim to develop talent, but nurture a lifelong love of the game. We offer a fun, caring environment where girls can develop their netball skills whilst making new friendships. We offer training on Saturday Mornings in Ruislip - Indoors in Autumn and Winter (Bishop Ramsey CoE School) and outdoors in the Summer (Queensmead Sports Centre).

Our training programs deliver exceptional quality in netball! Our coaches bring their A-game to every session upholding rigorous standards in coaching and refereeing to enusre players get the best guidance for their development. We field an U14 and an U15 team in the Queensnorth Junior Netball League. Matches take place on Sundays at Northwood College, Maxwell Road, Maxwell Rd, HA6 2YE.
