Welcome to Eagles Netball Club

Welcoming all new members

Diversity, fun and friendship

Eagles is a Club that loves and values everyone's differences. It doesn't matter whether you're a developing or experienced netballer - everyone is welcome here. The club is full of players from all kinds of backgrounds. It's a place that brings people together and makes them feel like they belong, all thanks to the game of netball.

Being part of Eagles is a lot of fun. It's not just about playing the game, it's about being part of a big group of friends. When we are practicing or playing matches, there's always lots of laughter and good times. The players help each other out and cheer each other on, even when things get tough. Eagles isn't just a netball club, it's a place where you can make friends and create happy memories that last a lifetime.
